# Slurm
  Interactive jobs:
    `srun --partition=mypartition --pty bash`
  or with other parameters:
    `srun --partition=mypartition --nodelist=node1 --gres=gpu:1 --mem=16G --pty bash`
  
  Batch jobs:
    `sbatch my_script.sh`
  
  
  Managing jobs:
  - view a list of all jobs:
    `squeue` or https://sc.stanford.edu
  - view detailed information for a specific job:
    `scontrol show job job_id`
  - cancel a job:
    `scancel "job_id"`
